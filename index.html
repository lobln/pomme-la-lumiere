Pomme

La lumière

pomme

Pomme (born 1996) is a French singer, musician, and songwriter from Lyon.

She began performing in local bars at the age of 16, developing a gentle folk and melancholic chanson style.

Her songs explore themes like love, death, nature, and queerness with poetic, minimalist lyrics and soft acoustic arrangements.
